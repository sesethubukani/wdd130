Our History

Founded in 1998, Whitewater Rafting Adventures has grown from a small local outfitter to one of the most trusted rafting companies in the region. Our mission has always been to provide safe and exciting rafting experiences that connect people with nature.

Our Adventure

We offer rafting trips ranging from calm beginner runs to thrilling whitewater expeditions. All of our guides are certified experts who prioritize safety while ensuring you have an unforgettable adventure.
